Forecasting Models & Methodologies
Linear Regression

Method: Fits a linear trend to recent price movements (60-day window)
Best For: Capturing consistent directional trends
Strength: Simple, interpretable, fast computation

Moving Average

Method: Uses 7-day vs 30-day moving average crossover signals
Best For: Short to medium-term trend following
Strength: Smooths out price volatility, trend identification

Exponential Smoothing

Method: Applies weighted averaging with exponential decay (α=0.3)
Best For: Adapting to recent price changes
Strength: Responsive to recent data, handles noise well

Polynomial Regression

Method: 2nd degree polynomial fitting on 50-day window
Best For: Capturing non-linear price patterns
Strength: Models acceleration/deceleration in trends

Seasonal Model

Method: Combines trend analysis with 7-day seasonality patterns
Best For: Stocks with recurring weekly patterns
Strength: Captures market behavior cycles

Ensemble Model

Method: Weighted combination of all successful individual models
Best For: Robust predictions with reduced overfitting
Strength: Balances strengths/weaknesses of all approaches

Model Selection Strategy: The application automatically runs all models and combines their predictions using a weighted ensemble approach. This reduces the risk of relying on a single model and provides more robust forecasts.

How to Upload Your Stock Data
Required CSV Format

Upload a CSV file with at least 100 data points for reliable forecasting. Your 5-year dataset will provide the best results for our 6-model ensemble forecasting system.

Required Columns
  • Date - Trading date (YYYY-MM-DD format)
  • Close - Closing price (required for forecasting)
  • Open - Opening price (optional, improves accuracy)
  • High - Daily high price (optional)
  • Low - Daily low price (optional)
  • Volume - Trading volume (optional)
Pro Tip: More data = better forecasts! Our models perform best with 2-5 years of historical data.

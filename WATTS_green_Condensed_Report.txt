# WATTS.green Comprehensive Analysis Report

## Fund Performance and Structure (as of Q4 2024)

WATTS.green Renewable Energy Fund I SCSp, SICAV-RAIF has demonstrated significant growth since its inception, with total commitments reaching €134 million following its second closing in Q4 2024. The fund's capital deployment strategy has been aggressive, with five capital calls executed since launch, totaling 34% of committed capital (€38.7 million). The fund maintains a strategic reserve of €31.2 million (27.5% of total commitments) for future opportunities.

### Investment Portfolio Composition
The fund's current portfolio represents a balanced mix of development stages and technologies:
- Onshore Wind (RTB): 27% of portfolio
- Onshore Wind (Repowering): 33% of portfolio
- Solar PV Projects: 40% of portfolio

### Capital Deployment Details
The fund has made four strategic investments to date:
1. Two onshore wind permitting projects
2. Two AgriSolar development platforms
3. Three operational onshore wind projects

Total capital called for investments as of December 31, 2024:
- Jans Lan: €10.0 million (8.8%)
- Galia Sol: €10.9 million (9.6%)
- Orient Platform: €10.3 million (9.0%)
- Green Neon: €24.9 million (21.9%)
- Agrisolar PV: €65.9 million (58.0%)
- Reserved Capital: €9.8 million (8.6%)

## Market Position and Strategy

### Geographic Focus
The fund maintains a strong focus on core European markets:
- Primary markets: France, Spain, and Benelux (80% of investments)
- Secondary markets: Other European Union countries (20% of investments)

### Investment Timeline
- Fund Launch: Q4 2023
- Second Closing: Q4 2024
- Target Final Closing: Q1 2025
- Investment Period: 5 years from First Closing Date (October 3, 2023)

### Fund Structure
- Vehicle Type: SICAV-RAIF (Luxembourg) – Special Limited Partnership (SCSp)
- Target Size: €500 million
- Minimum Commitment: At General Partner's discretion
- General Partner Contribution: 1.75% of Total Commitments (minimum €1 million)
- Fund Term: 10 years from Final Closing (with possibility of two one-year extensions)
- Management Fee: 2% per annum of Total Commitments
- Carried Interest: 20% with 7% hurdle rate and catch-up

## Pipeline and Market Opportunity

### French Market Potential
The French renewable energy market presents significant opportunities:
- Onshore Wind Capacity (Q3 2022): 20.4 GW
- Target Capacity by 2028: 33-35 GW
- Required Annual Installation Rate: 1.9 GW
- Historical Installation Rate: 1-1.5 GW per year

### Solar Market Development
The French solar market shows strong growth potential:
- Current Capacity (Q3 2022): 15.8 GW
- 2028 Target: 35.1-44 GW
- Ground-mount Target: 20.6-25 GW
- Required Annual Installation: 3 GW
- 2021 Achievement: 2.8 GW
- 2022 Projection: 2.5 GW

### European Repowering Opportunity
The fund has identified a significant opportunity in European wind repowering:
- Total Opportunity: 52 GW (turbines > 20 years old)
- EU Transition Investment Required: €1 trillion by 2030
